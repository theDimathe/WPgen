Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how BizFlowVibe uses cookies and similar technologies on bizflowvibe.life to support our community of US-based entrepreneurs.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and provide analytics insights.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies keep our member portal secure and functional.
  • Analytics cookies help us understand which resources and sessions are most valued.
  • Preference cookies save your language, session selections, and communication settings.

Managing Cookies

You can adjust cookie settings through your browser controls. Disabling certain cookies may limit access to protected resources or community features.
