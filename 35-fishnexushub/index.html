Who we are

Guiding anglers toward harmony with southern waters

FishNexusHub unites bilingual experts, conservationists, and traveling anglers across the United States who are drawn to the calm beauty of Chile. We pair practical know-how with a commitment to protect every lake, river, and fjord we touch.

Our mission

Protect marine ecosystems through informed journeys

We build awareness around responsible fishing methods, celebrate indigenous stewardship, and channel tourism dollars into restoration partners working from Valdivia to Punta Arenas.

  • Respect the catch: Promote catch-and-release for native trout and salmonids.
  • Support local science: Fund equipment for Chilean marine biology classrooms.
  • Empower explorers: Publish actionable checklists for US-based anglers heading south.
