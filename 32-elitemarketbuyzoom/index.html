Legacy

A timeline of elevated e-commerce craftsmanship

EliteMarketBuyZoom emerged from a heritage watch atelier and evolved into a digital-first collective devoted to precision-driven indulgence.

2016 — Boutique Origins

Opened a members-only loft in SoHo showcasing limited mechanical watches and bespoke device mods.

2019 — Digital Vault Launch

Released our immersive online vault, translating the tactile allure of premium materials into a seamless shopping interface.

2022 — Concierge Nationwide

Expanded white-glove delivery and on-site activation services to every major U.S. market.

2025 — Elite Sustainability

Introduced certified sourcing and circular refurbishing to preserve legacy pieces with minimal footprint.
